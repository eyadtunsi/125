Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best time to arrive for Hajj?

A: Ideally, 1–2 days before 8 Dhul-Hijjah so you can prepare and rest.

Q: What should I carry with me during the days in Mina, Arafat, and Muzdalifah?

A: Carry essential items like water, medication, identification, and a prayer mat.
