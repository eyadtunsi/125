Key Locations for Pilgrims

  • Masjid al-Haram (Makkah): Central mosque of Islam and the Kaaba.
  • Mina: Tent city where pilgrims stay during Hajj.
  • Arafat: Site of the central rite of Hajj (standing at Arafat).
  • Muzdalifah: Open area to spend the night after Arafat.
  • Jamarat: Site for the symbolic stoning of the devil.
